What the mark means

💬

The bubble

A single speech bubble stands in for every conversation format the app carries — feed post, story, chat, channel broadcast or video caption.

😊

The face

Two dots and a smile keep it warm and human — a super‑app built around real relationships, not just features.

🟡

The gold node

A small orbiting dot marks DukuDukuChat's home on the Pi Network — connected, without borrowing Pi's own logo or colours.

🎨

The gradient

Violet‑to‑coral matches the app's existing UI (login screen, buttons, accents) so the mark and the product feel like one system.

Do / don't

✓ Do

  • Keep the full gradient badge intact — don't flatten it to one colour.
  • Give it breathing room — at least a quarter of the icon's width as clear space.
  • Use the wordmark lockup for headers; the icon alone for nav bars, favicons and app icons.

✕ Don't

  • Don't recolour it, stretch it, or rotate it.
  • Don't place it on busy photo backgrounds without a solid tile behind it.
  • Don't combine it with Pi Network's own logo or mark — DukuDukuChat is on Pi, not of Pi.
flattened / greyed‑out — avoid
