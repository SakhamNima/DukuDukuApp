1. Who we are & scope of this policy

DukuDukuChat ("we", "us", "the app") is a social app — feed, stories, short video, chat, and Pi tipping — built on the Pi Network. This policy explains what personal data DukuDukuChat's backend collects when you use the app through the Pi Browser, how it's used, who it's shared with, and what control you have over it. It applies to the DukuDukuChat Pi-Browser app and its companion backend service.

By signing in with Pi Network and using DukuDukuChat, you agree to the collection and use of information as described here.

We deliberately do not collect more than the app needs to function — no third-party ad-tracking SDKs, no location tracking, no contacts-list upload, no device fingerprinting beyond what's needed to keep your session secure.

3. How we use your data

  • To create and secure your account, and confirm you're a real Pi Network Pioneer.
  • To operate the app's core features: showing your feed, stories, chats, and Discover videos; processing likes, comments, and Pi tips; running polls and mini-apps.
  • To keep the app safe — reviewing reports, enforcing blocks, and taking action against content or accounts that break our Community Guidelines.
  • To maintain and secure the service — debugging, preventing abuse, and keeping sessions authenticated.

We do not sell your personal data, and we do not use it to serve third-party advertising.

4. Third-party services

Pi Network. Signing in with Pi Network shares your Pi username and a Pi Network access token with our server, which we verify directly against Pi Network's own API (api.minepi.com) to confirm your identity. Any Pi payments you make or receive in DukuDukuChat are processed through the Pi Network payment platform, which has its own privacy practices — see Pi Network's own privacy policy for details on how Pi Network itself handles your data.

Hosting provider. Our backend and any media you upload (photos, videos, avatars) are stored on our hosting provider's infrastructure, used solely to run DukuDukuChat.

5. When we share data

  • With other users, as intended by the feature — e.g. your public posts, your display name/handle on comments, your profile info visible per your chosen audience setting.
  • With Pi Network, to authenticate you and process Pi payments.
  • If required by law — e.g. to comply with a valid legal request.
  • Never sold to advertisers or data brokers.

6. Data retention & deletion

We keep your account data for as long as your account is active. Stories are automatically removed after 24 hours (except for your own view of ones you posted). If you delete your account (via in-app settings, once available, or by contacting us — see Section 11), we delete your profile, posts, comments, chat messages you've sent, and uploaded media within 30 days, except where we're required to retain limited records (e.g. transaction logs) for legal or security reasons.

7. Security

Session authentication uses signed, HTTP-only cookies; passwords are never used since sign-in happens through Pi Network. Secret chats are gated behind an in-app passcode. We use standard web security practices (HTTPS in production, hashed/signed session tokens, rate limiting on authentication endpoints) — but no system is 100% secure, and we can't guarantee absolute security of information transmitted to the app.

8. Your rights & choices

  • Access & correction — you can view and edit your profile info directly in the app.
  • Blocking & reporting — you can block any account (Settings → Blocked accounts) and report posts, videos, chats, or accounts that violate our guidelines.
  • Passcode control — you can require a passcode for secret chats at any time.
  • Deletion — you can request full account and data deletion by contacting us (Section 11).

9. Children's privacy

DukuDukuChat is not directed at children and requires a verified Pi Network account to sign in. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we will remove it.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as DukuDukuChat's features evolve. We'll update the "Last updated" date above when we do, and material changes will be flagged in-app.
