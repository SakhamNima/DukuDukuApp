DukuDukuChat — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 12 July 2026

This is a starter template, not a substitute for legal advice. It was generated to unblock deployment and give reviewers (including the Pi Developer Portal) something accurate to read. Please review it, adjust it to match exactly what your app does, and consider having it checked by someone qualified before a public launch.

1. Who this covers

This policy applies to DukuDukuChat, a social app that lets you sign in with Pi Network, Google, or Apple, and use a feed, stories, short videos, chat, a Pi wallet, and related features.

2. Information we collect

CategoryWhat, and why
Account identityA provider-issued ID and display name from Pi Network, Google, or Apple, used to create and recognize your account. We do not receive your password from any provider.
Profile infoYour display name, handle, and avatar color, which you control from your profile settings.
Content you postPosts, stories, short videos, comments, poll votes, and chat messages you choose to send.
Wallet activityYour Pi balance and a history of tips/transfers made within the app.
Technical dataBasic request logs (e.g. IP address, timestamps) kept briefly for security and abuse prevention.
AI assistant textText you send to an AI feature (smart replies, rewrite, grammar check, translation, the chat assistant, captions/stickers). By default this is processed locally on our own server and not sent anywhere else. If the app owner has configured a third-party AI provider, that text is sent to the provider solely to generate the response.

3. How we use it

  • To operate core features: your feed, stories, chats, wallet, and notifications.
  • To verify who you are when you sign in, by checking your token directly with Pi Network, Google, or Apple.
  • To keep the app secure and prevent abuse (e.g. rate-limiting login attempts).

We do not sell your personal data, and we do not use it for third-party advertising.

4. Sign-in providers

When you sign in with Pi Network, Google, or Apple, that provider shares a limited identity token with us, which our server verifies directly with the provider. We only trust what the provider's own servers confirm — we never see or store your provider password.

5. Data retention & deletion

Your content and account data are kept for as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us using the details below.

6. Sharing

We do not share your personal data with third parties except: (a) the identity providers above, solely to verify sign-in; (b) if required by law.

7. Your choices

  • Control who sees each post or story via the audience setting (Public / Friends / Close Friends).
  • Enable a passcode lock on your profile from Settings.
  • Block, mute, or report other users from within a chat or profile.

8. Contact

Questions about this policy or your data can be sent to the app owner via the contact details listed on your DukuDukuChat account or app listing page.
